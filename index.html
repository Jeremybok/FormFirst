Welcome to FormFirst!

At FormFirst, we're focused on starting with the basics when it comes to improving as an athlete. We're opening a dedicated facility featuring an open space equipped with multiple cameras to precisely track and analyze the form of athletes across various sports. Our core mission is to meticulously track motions with advanced camera technology, compare them against professional techniques and best practices, and identify actionable improvements to perfect any athlete's form.
